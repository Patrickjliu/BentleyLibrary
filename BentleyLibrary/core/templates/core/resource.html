Online Databases

  • Academic Search Complete - A multi-disciplinary database of scholarly and general interest journals, books, and reports. If you're looking for background info on a topic, here is a great place to start!
  • JSTOR - A multi-disciplinary database that covers over 50 different disciplines/subject areas and contains over 1000 academic journal articles, photographs, editorials, and reviews.
  • New York Times - Use this link if you've already created an account/redeemed your school pass.
  • ProQuest Central - ProQuest gives us access to several academic databases, including books and journal articles. It covers many subjects, including history + political science, literature, and science.
  • Washington Post - Access to the daily edition of the Washington Post. You must be on campus to use this resource.
